Participants

Over the last decade and a half, Battle of Minds has transformed into a household label for thousand fo talented minds across the nation, giving them a platform to showcase their skills in innovation and strategy to solve complex real-world problems. Every year brings in new dimensions and experiences. The participants have stepped out of their comfort zone to revolutionize the restaurant scene in Dhaka, tackle Dhaka Traffic issue, leverage technology to meet SDGs and very recently develop Union Digital Centers (UDCs) for enhancing digital inclusion and literacy.

The indomitable success of Battle of Minds in Bangladesh led to its launch simultaneously across Asia Pacific, Middle East, Africa and Americas in 2018. National champions proceeded to face each other in a regional round and the three regional champions were then announced. In 2018, Team Bangladesh represented the region in the global final and winning the hearts of the elite panel, became the champion of the first ever global Battle of Minds.
